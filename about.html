About Us

Lane Kim Van Gerbig

Hailing from Stars Hollow, she is the only daughter of Mrs. Kim, owner of the beloved Kim’s Antiques. Locals call her the best female drummer since Sheila E. She is married to Zack and they have been sharing a new adventure together: raising their twin boys, Steve and Kwan!
Zack Van Gerbig

As founder and lead guitarist of Hep Alien, Zack has been rocking since 2002 with his best pal Brian. Aside from his love of rock and roll (and his sweetheart, Lane), he enjoys a good bluegrass jam session.
Brian Fuller

No one puts more groove into their bass guitar line than Brian! Though he is a self-ascribed “nerd,” he has been an integral part of Hep Alien since the beginning.
Gil

He may be a generation older than the rest of his bandmates, that doesn't stop Gil from rocking harder on his guitar than Hendrix at Woodstock. He also works part-time at his sandwich shop in downtown Hartford—stop by for 25% off Hep Alien tickets with every purchase of a meatball sub!
